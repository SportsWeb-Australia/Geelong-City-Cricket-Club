How these are built

All three share one content file (assets/gc-data.js) and one site script (assets/gc-site.js) that injects the nav, footer and every data-driven section. The only thing that differs between designs is theme.css — which is exactly the skin model: same content, same markup, different look.

Ten pages each: Home · About Us · Teams · Cricket Blast · Match Centre · Registration · Communications · News · Sponsors · Contact.

Sample data: ladder, fixtures, results and news are placeholders (clearly flagged in the UI) and swap to live SportsWeb One / PlayHQ feeds with no page changes. Everything else — teams, contacts, sponsors, registration links, Cricket Blast — is real, carried across from the club's site.
